Key Words and Concept:

  1. Century: one hundred years

  2. Discover: find out something for the first time

  3. Float: stay on top of a liquid

  4. Insect: small animal with six legs

  5. Rise: go higher; become more

  6. Sound: healthy; in good condition

  7. Suspend: to hang something from something else

  8. Wicker basket: basket made of long thin pieces of wood that are woven together

 
 

 Instructional Objectives:

1. Learning about the way man first started to fly.

2. Learning some new words such as century, float, rise, suspend, etc.

The Balloon   

Read the following passage as carefully and as fast as you can. You can also listen to a recording of this passage after doing the comprehension check.

Once, long age, the air belonged to the birds and insects. But in 1783 after centuries of talk about flying, man finally found a way to leave the ground, in that year it was discovered that a bag, or balloon, filled with hot air or with hydrogen gas would rise and float in the air.

The first passengers to go up, not by their own choice, were a sheep, a rooster, and a duck. They were suspended from the balloon in a wicker basket. When they came back safe and sound, men began to go up never sure where or when they would land.
