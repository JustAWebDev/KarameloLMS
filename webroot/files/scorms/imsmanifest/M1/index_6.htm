Key Words and Concept:

  1. Available: free and ready when you need it

  2. Desirable:  something that is desirable makes you want it

  3. Gently: kindly and softly

  4. Primary: first; earliest

  5. Wedding: marriage; ceremony when a man and woman marry

 
 

 

 Instructional Objectives:

1. Learning to use words in context.

2. Learning to distinguish between different parts of speech.

3. Learning some new words such as available, desirable, etc.
